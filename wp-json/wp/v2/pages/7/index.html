Principal’s Desk<\/h2>\n

 <\/p>\n

Apeejay School, Saket is not just a school but it is home away from home for our students, a place where they find a caring, stimulating environment where students are nurtured, challenged, educated and given every opportunity to reach their full potential. It\u2019s a place where unwavering belief in your child\u2019s potential allows them to build personally, socially and academically together with love, attention, respect, warmth, values and training for life. With the richness of our history, the diversity and extent of sister institutes, the wealth of experience of generations of alumni, Apeejay School, Saket strives to remain relevant to the present while preparing our Apeejayites for the future.<\/p>\n
